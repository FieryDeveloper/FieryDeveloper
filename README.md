### Compounding Disadvantage: Auditing Intersectional Bias in LLM-Generated Explanations Across Indian and American STEM Education

<sub>**A. Gupta**, N. Patil, S. Ghosh, S. Gaikwad · _arXiv:2601.14506_</sub>

> We audit how LLMs generate STEM explanations across **language, caste, and national context** — and find that disadvantages don't just add, they **compound**. Indian lower-caste learners asking in regional languages receive systematically lower-quality explanations than any single axis of bias would predict.
